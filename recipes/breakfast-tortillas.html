Ham and Cheese Breakfast Tortillas

This is great for a special brunch or even a quick and easy dinner. Other breakfast meats can be used, but the deli ham is the easiest since it is already fully cooked.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • 3 slices cooked ham, diced
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • ½ cup salsa

Steps

  1. Beat together eggs and milk.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled skillet (or non-stick pan) over medium high heat and cook egg mixture, stirring occasionally, until firm.
  3. When almost done, stir in ham, green onions, salt and pepper.
  4. Spoon 1/4 of the egg mixture on top of each tortilla.
  5. Sprinkle the eggs with cheese, and fold over the tortilla to make a roll.
  6. Top with salsa.
  7. Nom nom nom!
